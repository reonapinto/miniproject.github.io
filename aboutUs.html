About the Course

Welcome to the Front-End Web Development (FEWD) course! This course is designed to prepare you with the basic skills and knowledge needed to excel in the world of web development. Whether you're a beginner looking to kickstart your web development journey or an experienced developer aiming to enhance your front-end skills, this course has something to offer to everyone.

Throughout the FEWD course, you'll go through various aspects of front-end development, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and more.You'll learn how to create visually appealing and interactive web pages, ensuring that your websites are both functional and user-friendly. By the end of this course, you'll have the skills to create beautiful, responsive, and user-friendly websites, making you a valuable advantage in the ever-evolving field of web development.

About our mini Project

Our E-book website project is an exciting journey aimed at delivering a smooth and enjoyable podcast listening experience to our audience. In this project we've applied the skills and knowledge gained to create a dynamic platform for reader-friendly enthusiasts.

Key features of our E-book website include:

  • User-friendly interface.
  • Support Diverse Formats
  • Reader-friendly User Interface
  • Reading Tools Ideal for Online eBook Store
  • Reading Gamification
  • Store and Sync Favourite eBooks
  • Social Media Integrations

We're excited to present this project to you and hope you enjoy exploring the world of podcasts through our website.
